Toy Story 4
2019

Director Josh Cooley’s debut feature film is the fourth instalment in Pixar’s Toy Story series, which features the voice talents of Tom Hanks, Tim Allen, and Tony Hale. Woody, Buzz and co. are now the possessions of a young girl named Bonnie Anderson, who introduces a major complication into their lives when she turns a spork into a toy. Forky, however, sees himself as an eating utensil.

Characteristics
